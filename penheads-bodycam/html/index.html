Combined audio capture

Records your microphone plus desktop / game audio (what you hear — SFX and positional voice on your speakers). FiveM Lua cannot read PCM from the game or Mumble; capture runs in this browser panel via getUserMedia + getDisplayMedia.

Click Start so Chromium treats the next picker as a user gesture. Choose your monitor and enable Share audio (Windows).

Bodycam — monitor + game audio

Run bodycamclipaudio in the F8 console to open this panel. Then click below, select the monitor where FiveM is visible, and enable Share audio (Windows). A successful setup is remembered in this client until you run bodycamclipaudio_clear.

If you see Invalid state and no picker, your FiveM client build may not support screen capture from NUI (CEF limitation). Use ClipAudioCaptureMode mic in resource config, or setr bodycam_clip_audio_mode mic in server.cfg, so clips still record your microphone.
